Most people overestimate what they can do in a day but underestimate what they can do in a year. The secret? **Small habits.**

- Reading **5 pages daily** = 6+ books per year.
- Writing **one paragraph daily** = a full book in a year.
- Exercising **10 minutes daily** = a fit and healthy body over time.
